Lessons from the Top 10 Earners

We analyzed market leaders (Loom, Honey, Grammarly, GMass, CSS Scan) to build our framework:

  • Product-Led Growth (PLG): The product must act as its own marketing vehicle (e.g., instant value, watermarks/referrals).
  • Niche Dominance: Focus on solving one painful workflow for a specific demographic (Web Designers/Agencies).
  • Freemium/Free Trial: Remove friction to the "Aha!" moment.
  • Chrome Store ASO: 5-star reviews drive the majority of initial organic acquisition.
